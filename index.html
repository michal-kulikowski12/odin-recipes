Lasagna

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made of layered sheets of pasta, ricotta or béchamel sauce, meat (such as ground beef or pork), tomato sauce, and melted cheese, often topped with mozzarella. It is baked until golden and bubbly, creating a hearty and satisfying meal.

Spagetti

Spaghetti is a long, thin pasta typically made from durum wheat semolina. It is commonly served with a variety of sauces, such as marinara, Bolognese, or carbonara, and is often topped with grated cheese. It's a staple of Italian cuisine, loved for its simplicity and versatility.

Spiedini

Spiedini is an Italian dish consisting of skewered and grilled or roasted pieces of meat, often paired with vegetables. The meat can include beef, chicken, lamb, or pork, and it's typically marinated in herbs, olive oil, and spices before cooking. Spiedini is often served as a main course, accompanied by side dishes like salad, bread, or roasted potatoes. It's popular in Italian regions like Sicily and Naples.
